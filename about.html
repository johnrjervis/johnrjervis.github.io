About JJ's Juggling videos

The origins of this website go back to a series of juggling videos recorded and sent to my family during the first lockdown of the Covid-19 pandemic.

Time spent in quarantine hotels lead me to record further videos and to start to learn web development. I combined these activities to launch this site in April 2021.

Since then, I've added another video from another stay in hotel quarantine, and made changes to the site design and layout during my spare time.
